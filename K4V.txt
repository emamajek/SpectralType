K4V

Garrison94 does not list a K4V anchor standard, and MK73 does not list
a K4V dagger standard. Only two stars in the Bright Star Catalog (5th
ed.)  are classified as K4V (HR 5568 = HD 131977, HR 8721 = HD
216803). The primary (GJ 689 = HD 160964), secondary (GJ 879 = HD
216803 = TW PsA), and tertiary (GJ 570A = HD 131977) standards all
have approximately solar [Fe/H] values, so the ~solar metallicity mean
properties for K4V are well-defined.

Morgan65 classified two Hyads as K4V: HD 285720 & HD 285742. Pecaut16
finds that Houk/Michigan stars classified as "K3/4V" map well to "K4V"
among the classifications of Gray03/06, and Houk/Michigan "K4V" maps
roughly to "K4+V" on the Gray03/06 (note the Gray classifications use
the Keenan standards).
