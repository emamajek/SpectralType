G5V 

Garrison94 lists HD 20630 (=kap^1 Cet) as the G5V anchor standard, but MK73 does not list a G5V dagger standard. kap^1 Cet and mu Cas have remained the main G5V standards going back to MK43 through Keenan89. Historically, the 1922 Report of the IAU Committee on Stellar Classification mentioned mu Her as the "example" of a dG5 dwarf star, but shortly after it was considered a subgiant.
