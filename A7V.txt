Garrison94 does not list a A7V anchor standard, and MK73 does not list
a A7V dagger standard. Garrison96 lists only two A7V stars among the
brightest ~300 stars on the sky (V<3.55mag): alf Aql (A7Vnn) and alf
Cep (A7Van).

Probably the best A7V standards are 21 LMi (HD 87696) and Altair (HD 187642).
