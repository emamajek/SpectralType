  (see mags in Gezari99).  Astrometric solution in USNO Bright Star
  Catalog (UBSC; Zacharias22) has high chi2, although Kervella22
  Hipparcos+Gaia estimates less significant tangential velocity

  map for its position/distance. TIC/Stassun19 has it as R=100.465Rsun
  giant with Teff=3780+-122K, which seems
