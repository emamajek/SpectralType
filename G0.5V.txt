  One of the G0V standards with the longest history and continuity,
  but Keenan89 switched subtype in his last compilation.  WDS lists it
  as a tight binary (Hummel95) - basically a resolved SB2 (P=10.02d).
  WDS lists mag1=5.0, mag2=6.9, so del(mag) = 1.9.
  Teff=5920K(Gray01), logg=4.31(Gray01), [M/H]=-0.21(Gray01),
  E(b-y)=0.02(Gray01). PASTEL compilation shows that most metallicity
  estimates are between [Fe/H] ~ -0.5 and -0.2, so clearly metal poor.

  nearly the same designation: the *solar twin* pi01 UMi A (HD 139777)

  a "solar twin" as it is ~80K hotter, younger (~0.5Gyr) with higher
