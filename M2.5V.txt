GJ 381 should probably be considered the anchor given its
perigree with Kirkpatrick/Henry/Hawley/Reid, but GJ 250B
is also strong.

Radii for M2.5Vs span ~0.30-0.46 Rsun. I adopt the Mann15 Radius
vs. M_Ks trend for [Fe/H]=0.0 (for M_Ks=6.26) to help guide estimation
of the mean HRD position.
