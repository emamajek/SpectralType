M2V

Kirkpatrick(in GrayCorbally09) call Gl 411 the primary M2V
standard. It has very strong pedigree as it is considered M2V by
JohnsonMorgan53, Houk, Keenan, Kirkpatrick, Henry, Hawley, Reid.

As with the other mid-M types, there is a wide range of radii among
even the M2V standards and exemplars: approximately 0.39-0.52 Rsun. I
adopt the Mann15 calibration for M_Ks vs Radius for [Fe/H] (adopting
M_Ks=5.98) to guide estimation of the mean HRD point.
