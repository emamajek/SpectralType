F3III

7 Sgr and 20 CVn have been considered F3III standards, but neither is very good. 

MorganAbt72 standard:  7 Sgr (=HR 6724) - F3III
MK78 standard:	       20 Cvn (=HD 115604) - F3III
		       63 Tau - kA2,mF3III 

There are no "F3III" stars in Gray's papers. 

# No standards

MK43, JM53, Morgan65, Houk, GrayNstars, and GrayCorbally10 do not list
a F3III standard.
