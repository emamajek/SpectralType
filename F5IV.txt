  appears to be a large scatter in quoted temperature subtypes and
  luminosity classes. It is probably more consistent with IV-V or IV
  luminosity class in terms of its gravity and absolute magnitudes, as
  it is ~0.64mag above the MS, log(g)~3.9, and its most quoted

  1970s are consistent with F5. While Gray flags it as very
