Note that Rabus19 points out a discontinuity in the region
Teff=3200K-3340K where M dwarf radii range from 0.18 to 0.42
Rsun. M3.5V (Teff ~ 3260K) is right in the middle of this region, and
indeed there are interferometric radii for M3.5Vs ranging from ~0.20
to ~0.37 Rsun. Mann15 fits a trend through the middle of the swarm,
and I adopt his [Fe/H] fit to guide the mean HRD point for M3.5V as
function of M_Ks.
