M5V

Kirkpatrick(in GrayCorbally09) calls Gl 51 (= Wolf 47) as the primary
M5V standard. 

Johnson & Morgan 1953 (JM53) discussing updates to classifications
since MKK43: "A few standard M dwarfs have been reclassified with
fixed point that Barnard's star is set at M5V." However they do not
actually list Barnard's star among the standards (their Table 2). For
whatever reason, this did not stick and on most classifications since
the 1970s, Barnard's star is considered M4V.  Somewhat surprisingly,
Keenan84 does not list M5 as a full subtype like M4 and M6.
