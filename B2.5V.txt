  (11/25/2012) => (B-V)o=-0.192, (U-B)o=-0.713, E(B-V)=0.047,
  Av=0.153.  Hauck97 photometry dereddened following Castelli91:
  (b-y)o=-0.111, c0=0.248, E(b-y)=0.050.  Teffs: 17579K(EEM,Hauck97
  phot.=>Napiwotzki93), 17657K(U-Bo=>Bessell98), 18478K(EEM,Hauck97
  phot=>BalonaShobbrook84 calibration), 18508K(EEM,Hauck97 phot. =>
  Balona94) => median Teff = 18068K. If it is at 420 pc (typical 
  Ori OB1 distance), then Mv = -2.02.
